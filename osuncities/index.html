CITIES IN OSUN STATE, NIGERIA

Osun (/ˈoʊʃuːn/) is an inland state in south-western Nigeria. Its capital is Osogbo. It is bounded in the north by Kwara State, in the east partly by Ekiti State and partly by Ondo State, in the south by Ogun State and in the west by Oyo State (from Wikipedia).
